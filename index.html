What is ASTRAI?

ASTRAI (Advanced Supernova Transient Research with Artificial Intelligence) is a research initiative developing tools for the automatic characterisation of Supernovae using advanced Artificial Intelligence and synthetic datasets. The project brings together physics-based modelling and machine learning to help scientists make better use of the growing volume of astronomical data.
